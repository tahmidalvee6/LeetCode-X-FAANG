## 💡 Tips for FAANG Interviews

> **Think out loud** — interviewers care about your process, not just the answer.

1. **Understand before coding** — Spend the first 5 minutes clarifying the problem
2. **Start with brute force** — Then optimize step by step
3. **Know your complexities** — Always state time and space complexity
4. **Practice on a whiteboard** — Simulates real interview conditions
5. **Review failed problems** — Understand *why* your approach was wrong
6. **Mock interviews** — Use platforms like Pramp, Interviewing.io
